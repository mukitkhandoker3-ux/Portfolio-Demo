About Me

Shaping Sound,
Defining Stories

I am Mukit Khandoker — a professional sound designer and location sound recordist based in Bangladesh, with over 4 years of hands-on experience in the field.

From capturing pristine dialogue on a film set to sculpting immersive soundscapes in post-production, I live and breathe audio. Every click, rumble, breath, and silence is a narrative decision.

My work spans documentary films, narrative features, short films, commercials, and original music compositions — always with the same obsession: making sound invisible yet unforgettable.

What I Do

🎙

Location Sound Recording

Professional on-set audio capture using boom microphones, wireless lav systems, and multi-track field recorders. Every line, every ambient breath — captured clean.

Sound Design

Music Composition

Original score composition tailored to visual storytelling — from orchestral textures to electronic atmospheres that breathe life into picture.

Dialogue editing, ADR supervision, noise reduction, and full mix for broadcast, cinema, and online delivery specs.

My Process

01

Listen First

Before a single mic goes up, I study the script, storyboard, or brief with fresh ears. Understanding the story's emotional core shapes every subsequent audio decision.

02

Scout & Prepare

Location surveys, RF spectrum scans, and gear prep — walking into a set without knowing the acoustic challenges is amateur hour. I never wing it.

03

Capture Clean

On location, I prioritise clean, production-ready audio. Every take is monitored critically. The post team shouldn't need to fix what production should have nailed.

04

Design & Sculpt

In post, the real world becomes raw material. I layer, sculpt, pitch, and manipulate sound until the acoustic reality matches the cinematic one.

05

Mix to Picture

The final mix is where everything becomes invisible — dialogue sits perfectly, music breathes around it, and effects anchor the world without announcing themselves.
